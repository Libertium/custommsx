Frequency Tone
Ft = (Fclock/16)/TP
Fclock = Input Clock Frequency
TP - Tone Period (TP(11)..TP(0)) [R1:R0];[R3:R2];[r5:R4]
                                    A       B       C
Frequency Noise
Fn = (Fclock/16)/TN
TN = NP(4)..NP(0)  [R6]

Frequency Envelope
Fe = (Fclock/256)/EP
EP = EP(15)..EP(0) [R14:R13]
